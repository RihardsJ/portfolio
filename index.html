myStory

Back in 2008 I have “Riga State Technical School” as a programming engineer. However, as you remember banks messed up something and it was not the best time to look for a job. I decided to take pack my bags and fly to UK to visit my aunty and look for opportunities. Somerset is not a Silicon Valley and my English was far from being perfect so I ended up working in a restaurant just to cover my expenses. For few years I was enjoying afternoon teas and Sunday roasts but somehow, I was not happy with what I was doing. One day I decided to hit the road again and look for better opportunities in the capital. I missed calculated my expenses and ended up spending all my money for the rent. To be sure that I have food in my fridge I started to work in bars (apparently, I am making great espresso martinis ☺). Martinis are cool but still my childhood dream is living in me. I started to do some C++ coding lessons online just to remember good old days and few month ago I bought a laptop and used my old-warrior to install Linux just to remember how it works. Online lessons are great but I feel that I need some guidance and mentoring to further develop my-self. Unfortunately, the virus arrived and most of things has stopped all over but this time I will not change my direction 💪.

Founders and Coders

First of all, I would like to thank Gregor and Yvonne as well as the rest of the Founders and Coders team for the great month that I have had so far. It is an absolute pleasure to do pair programming. Just to be accepted for pre-course is already exciting thing that I was waiting for many years to happen.

I like the idea of Founders and Coders and I believe as a bootcamp it really stands out in comparison to others. While most of the bootcamps representing an idea of having a quick course with a job at the end Founders and Coders are highly focused on learning and development. The learning process is highly emphasized and that is what I am looking for. If I am ready to take a job in web development one day that`s great. However, at the moment I am to building skills and knowledge in the field. Therefore, I believe FAC is the best place to continue my learning. The sweetest part is to have a chance to teach others once I have learned my-self.

“Those who know, do. Those that understand, teach.”
― Aristotle
